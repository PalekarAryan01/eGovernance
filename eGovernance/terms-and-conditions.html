Terms and Conditions for Admission

Admission Requirements

The student must meet certain requirements, including:

  • Providing all required documents.
  • Paying the required admission fee.
  • Satisfying the admission requirements of the college.

Responsibility of the Student

The student is responsible for ensuring that all information provided to the college is accurate and true. Misrepresentation of facts or provision of false documents is strictly prohibited.

Consequences of Failing to Meet Requirements

The college reserves the right to:

  • Cancel the student's admission if they fail to meet the specified requirements.
  • Retain fees already paid if false information or forged documents are provided.
